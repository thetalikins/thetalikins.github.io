When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?


So far, I've noticed I get particularly triggered around two types of experience: the firist and perhaps most prevalent is when things aren't working as I expect them to. Sometimes I catch myself saying there's a needle in a haystack in term of finding the broken piece of code. I can work through these feelings by cooling off, taking breaks, and asking for help. The second way I notice myself getting triggered in code so far is when I am truly stumped, and procure help, I tend to think of myself as a fraud. This week more than others, I've felt a growing fear about my future as a coder. There are lots of things I'm passioante about in this life, and while I'm sure there's more intriuge to discover as I get more literate, I'm aware of a sense of comparison to some of my cohort members, which often leads to stories of work scarcity, self-abandonment, and generalized fear about the future.
